The Galway Clinic is a state of the art 146 bed hospital which provides 24 hour health care services. There are in excess of 140 highly skilled Physicians and Surgeons covering a wide variety of specialties. The Galway Clinic introduced vital services such as Oncology treatment and Cardiac services to the West of Ireland in 2004. The Galway Clinic provides the widest range of treatment for prostate cancer in Ireland. The Galway Clinic provides the latest technologies in Radiology Imaging with services including 3T MRI, Cardiac CT, PET CT, Digital Mammography, CT, Ultrasound and X-Ray.
